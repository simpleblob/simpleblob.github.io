Book Summary - Positive Workplace Politics

creation date: 2016-12-15, latest update: 2016-12-15

Politics is an unofficial and often behind the scene channels to sell ideas, influence an organization, increase power or achieve other objectives.

Important decision making – should lobby everyone to same "big picture" conclusion prior to the big meeting. The meeting itself is just a "ceremony" to go over details and commit to the decision.
