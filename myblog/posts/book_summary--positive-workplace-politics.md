Politics is an unofficial and often behind the scene channels to sell
ideas, influence an organization, increase power or achieve other
objectives.

Important decision making -- should lobby everyone to same \"big
picture\" conclusion prior to the big meeting. The meeting itself is
just a \"ceremony\" to go over details and commit to the decision.
